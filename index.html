SAGE University Bhopal is a provoking learning institute in the educational hub Bhopal. It has a total of 11 departments i.e. Advanced Computing, Agriculture, Art, Humanities, & Social Sciences, Commerce, Computer Application, Design, Engineering & Technology, Journalism and Mass Communication, Management, Performing Arts, and Sciences. The institute provides a total of 80 courses out of which 39 are UG courses and 41 are PG courses.

The courses offered by SAGE University Bhopal are UG and PG. At UG level, a total of 11 courses including BA, B.Com., B.Sc., B.Sc Hons in agriculture, B.Tech (Advanced Computing), B.Tech (Engg. & Tech.), BCA, BJMC, B.Des., BBA, and BPA.

  • At the PG level, it offers 10 courses including MA, M.Com, M.Sc, MJMC, M.Tech (Advanced Computing), M.Tech (Engg. & Tech.), MCA, MBA, M.Des., and MPA.
  • Students who secure at least 50% in their higher secondary education programs are eligible to apply for UG courses. For the PG courses, they must have a Bachelor's degree in a relevant subject. However, even for the PG courses, 50% of marks in 10+2 is mandatory to be eligible.
  • The admission to all the courses will be purely based on the marks obtained by the students in SAGE Entrance Exam - SEE.
  • The application form of SEE can be filled online on the SEE portal.

How to Apply for SAGE University Bhopal?

The selection of all the PG courses will be based on marks obtained by the students in SEE conducted by the SAGE University.

The admission to all the programs at SAGE University Bhopal is based on the entrance examination conducted by the SAGE University itself. The entrance exam is known as SAGE Entrance Exam (SEE). To apply for the courses at SAGE University Bhopal, candidates will be required to apply for SEE 2021. Students can apply online on the SEE portal.

The process is as follows:

  • Step 1: Registration: Register for SEE 2021 on the SEE portal.
  • Step 2: Application Form: Fill the application form with all the mandatory details i.e. personal, family, educational.
  • Step 3: Online Payment: Candidates will have to make the payment of INR 1600/- through online payment gateway i.e. credit card/debit card/ UPI/ wallet / saved card, etc.
  • Step 4: Upload the prescribed documents.
  • Step 5: Submit the application form.

Documents Required:

  1. The candidates have to report to the university with the following documents:
  2. Any one ID Proof (Voter ID/Driving License/PAN Card/Aadhar/Bank Pass Book).
  3. 10th Mark Sheet (Original) with 2 Sets of Xerox Copy.
  4. 12th Mark Sheet (Original)/Online Print (If Mark Sheet is not available) with 2 Sets of Xerox Copy.
  5. Graduation degree certificate/Mark Sheet (Original)/Online Print (If Mark sheet is not available) with 2 Sets of Xerox Copy (all semester) [For PG courses].
  6. Migration (Original) with 2 Sets of Xerox Copy.
  7. Two recent coloured passport size photographs.
  8. Original Registration Payment Receipt.

SAGE University Bhopal Scholarships

SAGE University Bhopal offers a wide range of Scholarships to deserving and talented students. Moreover, the institute also offers scholarships to the students who secure high achievements in sports at State and National levels.

Merit-based scholarship: It is offered to the students who secure a position in the top three students in class 12th in the respective boards i.e. CBSE/ ISC / State Boards.

MP Government Scholarship: This scholarship is applicable to the students belonging to SC/ ST/ OBC/ Minority category etc. The scholarship is administered by the Government based on the annual income of the family.

Scholarship Selection Criteria:

  1. The scholarship application form should be submitted by the applicant to the University office after getting admission.
  2. The eligible candidates desirous of scholarship are required to fill the scholarship form during the admission process.
  3. The scholarship amount will be calculated only on tuition fees and is applicable for 1st year only.
  4. The students fulfilling more than one eligibility criteria will be considered only as per higher preference mentioned in the scheme and will be eligible for only one type of scholarship in a year.
